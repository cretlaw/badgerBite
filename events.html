FUTRA presents KINETIC

Date: August 11 and 12 at @jason_vass Gallery

Location: Jason Vass Gallery LA Arts District

On Saturday, August 11th, FUTRA sets into motion KINETIC, a two-day multimedia event featuring 40 visual artists, performers, and musicians at the JASON VASS GALLERY in the Arts District of DTLA. On Saturday entry is $5 before 8pm and $10 after. Sunday entry is $5 all day. KINETICS is movement. It is a symptom of life embedded in the grammar of our humanity, our constantly evolving nature and expression. Kinetic Art shocks the unflinching static of traditional forms by breaking into a radical new dimension… time. Through concepts of motion, or virtual and apparent movement, it defibrillates art by rousing life into the medium. The dynamic relationship between reality and illusion is kinetic, and at times, the two become one. Join us and propel into the KINETIC revolution – where we present, rather than represent, our own living reality. Creative concepts, striking visuals, vibrations and performances will be featured both Saturday evening and Sunday afternoon. The two-day event is 21+ with a full cash bar.

Futra presents Super Future

On Saturday, July 29th, Futra presents Super Future, a one-day multimedia event featuring over 40 visual artists, performers, and musicians at the LOT 613 warehouse gallery. Futra has seamlessly fused electronic music and visual art throughout its five-year orbit in Los Angeles. Originally producing club events, the focus and scope of Futra has altered to generating large-scale, concept-driven interactive experiences.

In its most visionary show to-date, SUPER FUTURE enters into the awakening of our electronic prophecies. The idea manifests both the specter and reverie of our imagined future. Immerse yourself in an evening of retro-future and future-forward artwork- integrating avant-garde art and design, computers, circuits, artificial intelligence, imagined realities, cyber fashion, alternate landscapes, and re-purposed, hacked, experimental and new technology. SUPER FUTURE fashion is encouraged.
